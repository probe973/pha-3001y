Converting Metric Units - Solutions

Solutions to Converting Metric Units Questions

Below are the step-by-step solutions for converting metric units.

Solutions

1. Convert $785000\text{mcg}$ to $\text{g}$.

$1\text{g} = 1,000,000\text{mcg}$

$785000 \div 1000000 = 0.785$

Answer: $\textbf{0.785\text{g}}$

2. Convert $0.0053\text{g}$ to $\text{mg}$.

$1\text{g} = 1000\text{mg}$

$0.0053 \times 1000 = 5.3$

Answer: $\textbf{5.3\text{mg}}$

3. Express $0.0028\text{mol}$ in $\text{mmol}$.

$1\text{mol} = 1000\text{mmol}$

$0.0028 \times 1000 = 2.8$

Answer: $\textbf{2.8\text{mmol}}$

4. Express $4.2 \times 10^4 \text{mL}$ in $\text{L}$.

$1\text{L} = 10^3 \text{mL}$

$(4.2 \times 10^4) \div 10^3 = 4.2 \times 10^{(4-3)}$

$4.2 \times 10^1 = 42$

Answer: $\mathbf{4.2 \times 10^1 \text{L}}$

5. Convert $1.5\text{L}$ to $\text{mL}$.

$1\text{L} = 1000\text{mL}$

$1.5 \times 1000 = 1500$

Answer: $\textbf{1500\text{mL}}$

6. Convert $1250\text{mg}$ to $\text{g}$.

$1\text{g} = 1000\text{mg}$

$1250 \div 1000 = 1.25$

Answer: $\textbf{1.25\text{g}}$

7. Convert $0.00006\text{kg}$ to $\text{mg}$.

$1\text{kg} = 1,000,000\text{mg}$

$0.00006 \times 1000000 = 60$

Answer: $\textbf{60\text{mg}}$

8. Express $7.5 \times 10^{-2}\text{g}$ in $\text{mg}$.

$1\text{g} = 10^3 \text{mg}$

$(7.5 \times 10^{-2}) \times 10^3 = 7.5 \times 10^{(-2+3)}$

$7.5 \times 10^1 = 75$

Answer: $\mathbf{7.5 \times 10^1 \text{mg}}$

9. Convert $980\text{mL}$ to $\text{L}$.

$1\text{L} = 1000\text{mL}$

$980 \div 1000 = 0.98$

Answer: $\textbf{0.98\text{L}}$

10. Express $3.1 \times 10^6 \text{mcg}$ in $\text{g}$.

$1\text{g} = 1,000,000\text{mcg}$

$(3.1 \times 10^6) \div 10^6 = 3.1 \times 10^{(6-6)}$

$3.1 \times 10^0 = 3.1$

Answer: $\mathbf{3.1 \times 10^0 \text{g}}$

11. Convert $5500000\text{mg}$ to $\text{kg}$.

$1\text{kg} = 1,000,000\text{mg}$

$5500000 \div 1000000 = 5.5$

Answer: $\textbf{5.5\text{kg}}$

12. Express $2.5 \times 10^{-3}\text{L}$ in $\text{mL}$.

$1\text{L} = 10^3 \text{mL}$

$(2.5 \times 10^{-3}) \times 10^3 = 2.5 \times 10^{(-3+3)}$

$2.5 \times 10^0 = 2.5$

Answer: $\mathbf{2.5 \times 10^0 \text{mL}}$
