1. $((22 \times 6) - 15) \div 3$

$22 \times 6 = 132$

$132 - 15 = 117$

$117 \div 3 = 39$

Answer: $\textbf{39}$

2. $((512 - 128) \times 2) \div 64$

Answer: $\textbf{12}$
