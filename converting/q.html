Converting Metric Units

Instructions

  • Calculators of any description are not allowed.
  • All answers should be written to 2 decimal places (unless requested otherwise).
  • All answers must include units, where appropriate. Answers without units (or incorrect units) will be marked as incorrect.
  • If using scientific notation, use as a number between 1 & 10 e.g. $1.6 \times 10^3$

Questions

1. Convert $785000\text{mcg}$ to $\text{g}$.

2. Convert $0.0053\text{g}$ to $\text{mg}$.

3. Express $0.0028\text{mol}$ in $\text{mmol}$.

4. Express $4.2 \times 10^4 \text{mL}$ in $\text{L}$.

5. Convert $1.5\text{L}$ to $\text{mL}$.

6. Convert $1250\text{mg}$ to $\text{g}$.

7. Convert $0.00006\text{kg}$ to $\text{mg}$.

8. Express $7.5 \times 10^{-2}\text{g}$ in $\text{mg}$.

9. Convert $980\text{mL}$ to $\text{L}$.

10. Express $3.1 \times 10^6 \text{mcg}$ in $\text{g}$.

11. Convert $5500000\text{mg}$ to $\text{kg}$.

12. Express $2.5 \times 10^{-3}\text{L}$ in $\text{mL}$.
